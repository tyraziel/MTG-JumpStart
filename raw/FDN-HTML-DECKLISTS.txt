<p>The Beginner Box contains ten themed <em>Jumpstart </em>decks, two of which (the Vampires and Cats decks) are specifically ordered for the tutorial. These decks are not to be shuffled. If they become out of order and need to be reordered, don't worry! The tutorial order for the tutorial decks is <a href="#Tutorial">right here</a>, so you can go through that experience whenever you like.</p>
