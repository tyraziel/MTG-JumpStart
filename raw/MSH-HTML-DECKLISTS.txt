<p><i>Magic: The Gathering</i> | <i>Marvel Super Heroes</i> Jumpstart Booster boxes contain 24 Jumpstart Boosters with at least 1 new-to<i>-Magic</i> card from MSC in each booster. Each Jumpstart Booster also contains 1 full-art city chaos or city calm basic land.</p>

<p>To play, open two Jumpstart Boosters and shuffle them together for a complete deck. It's as easy as that! Jumpstart Boosters are perfect for players who are learning to play or want to expand their collection with cards from across the Marvel Universe. With 51 different booster themes, there's something for every player.</p>

<p>Check out all the cards from Jumpstart Boosters in the <a href="https://magic.wizards.com/en/products/marvel/marvel-super-heroes/card-image-gallery" target="_blank"><i>Magic: The Gathering</i> | <i>Marvel Super Heroes</i> Card Image Gallery</a>. Below, you can find all the booster themes from <i>Magic: The Gathering</i> | <i>Marvel Super Heroes</i> Jumpstart Boosters. The set releases on June 26 and is available for preorder now from <a href="https://locator.wizards.com/" target="_blank">your local game store</a>, <a href="https://partner.tcgplayer.com/e1d7gz" target="_blank">TCGplayer</a>, <a href="	https://gtly.ink/bdrkd9WKTp" gtm-event="retailer_cta_click" gtm-link-text="Amazon" gtm-section="Daily Article" target="_blank">Amazon</a>, and elsewhere <i>Magic</i> is sold.</p>
